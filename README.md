## Tips for Playing

### KOF Fighting
1. **Energy Management**: Don't spam attacks - save energy for special moves
2. **Blocking**: Hold block to reduce incoming damage
3. **Combos**: Chain consecutive hits to multiply damage
4. **Spacing**: Use movement to avoid opponent attacks
5. **Character Selection**: Different characters have different speeds and jump heights

### Mario Platforming
1. **Collect 9 Coins**: Required to win the level
2. **Use Checkpoints**: They auto-save your progress
3. **Manage Power-ups**: Use them wisely to survive enemy encounters
4. **Platform Timing**: Jump carefully on narrow or dangerous platforms
5. **Enemy Patterns**: Different enemies move at different speeds
